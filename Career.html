Career

We Are Hiring !!

At Stanza we give a chance to young energetic people across the country. Once you enter the organization, there is a well-defined growth path that is mentored by senior managers. The culture to perform is deeply ingrained in our people’s philosophy. The organization lays emphasis on mandatory on-the-job training, value-added external training, and faculty improvement programs.

Open Positions

+ Sales Executive (Exp. 2-3 years)

Job Description


Job Profile: Sales Executive

Experience: Min 2-3 years

Qualification: Graduation

Joining: Immediate

Salary: As per Company Standard

Location: Noida

Logistics Sales Executive Responsibilities/Duties:

  • Cold-calling, generating new leads and prospecting accounts
  • Analyzing consumer needs and developing innovative solutions
  • Educating customers about our shipping solutions
  • Conducting research on customers that have a need for our services
  • Qualifying prospects for size, complexity, and overall fit for our company
  • Using Salesforce automation tools to track progress and report goals
  • Grow revenue and relationships with existing customer base
  • Communicate effectively with customers, shippers, and receivers regarding changes in pick-up/delivery and potential service failures
  • Exceed all standards for prospecting calls, presentations, proposals, and closes
  • Provide documented activity reports of your sales efforts on a daily, weekly, and monthly basis
  • Negotiate contracts and rate agreements
  • Support carrier reps with the coordination of transportation freight in a cost-effective manner
  • Document issues with orders thoroughly

About Us

Your Trusted Global Logistics Partner

Stanza Shipping (India) Pvt Ltd was founded as part of the Shobha Group, a company with a rich legacy of over 60 years in international trade. To continue and expand this legacy into new business ventures, When our founder, Sarthak Gupta, sought a logistics provider to match the high standards of customer service and transparency required for his family's trading company, he couldn’t find one in the market. After working with several logistics firms that didn’t meet his expectations, he decided to create a company that prioritizes customer satisfaction, transparency, and cutting-edge information systems.

Our Mission

We are committed to creating a logistics experience that is reliable, transparent, and built on strong customer relationships.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide innovative logistics solutions that enable businesses to thrive in a competitive global market.
